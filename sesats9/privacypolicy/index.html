Privacy Policy

SESATS 9 is intended as a local study resource. This app does not require an account, and it does not ask you to submit personal information in order to review questions, answers, critiques, or your scorecard.

Information Stored by the App

The app stores your progress on device so it can remember which questions you answered correctly or incorrectly. This includes your selected answers and review state. That data stays on your device unless you remove the app or reset the local database inside the app.

Personal Information

SESATS 9 does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location through the core study experience.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services to profile you or monitor your activity across other apps or websites.

Internet Access

Some parts of the app may present web-based content, such as policy or informational pages. If future features require internet-based services, this policy should be updated to reflect those changes.

Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time as the app changes. Updated policy text will be made available within the app.
