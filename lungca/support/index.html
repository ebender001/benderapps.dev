Lung Cancer Stage

Welcome to the support page for Lung Cancer Stage. This app is designed as a quick-reference educational tool for TNM-based lung cancer stage grouping.

App Purpose

Lung Cancer Stage helps users select T, N, and M categories and view the corresponding overall stage. The app also provides quick access to TNM definitions for convenient reference.

Important Medical Disclaimer

Lung Cancer Stage is provided for educational and reference purposes only. It is not a substitute for clinical judgment, multidisciplinary review, official staging manuals, pathology review, radiology review, or oncology consultation. Users should confirm staging decisions with current AJCC / IASLC source material and other authoritative clinical resources.

What the App Includes

  • TNM category selection
  • Automatic stage display
  • T, N, and M definition lookup
  • Fast point-of-care educational reference design

Compatibility

The app is intended for supported iPhone devices running a current version of iOS. App behavior, appearance, and icon styles may vary depending on iOS version and Home Screen appearance settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the stage determined?

The app uses the selected T, N, and M categories to display the corresponding overall stage grouping. Users should verify final staging using official reference materials.

Does the app provide medical advice?

No. The app is an educational and reference tool only and does not provide diagnosis, treatment advice, or individualized medical recommendations.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email your comments to support@benderapps.dev. Include your device, iOS version, and a brief description of the issue.

What source is used for staging?

The app references AJCC / IASLC 9th edition staging concepts as presented within the app. Final clinical use should always be confirmed against official current source documents.
