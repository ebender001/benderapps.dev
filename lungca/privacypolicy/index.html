Privacy Policy

Lung Cancer Stage

This Privacy Policy explains how Lung Cancer Stage accesses, uses, and handles information, including health-related information entered into the app, when you use the app.

Summary

Lung Cancer Stage is an educational and reference app that allows users to enter lung cancer staging-related information in order to calculate or review stage classifications. The app may access health-related information that a user manually enters into the app, such as tumor staging characteristics or other cancer-related clinical details. This information is used only to provide the app’s staging and reference functions.

Lung Cancer Stage does not create user accounts and, unless you separately contact support by email, does not transmit this health-related information to Bender Apps, LLC. Health-related information entered into the app is processed locally on the device and is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Health Data Accessed in the App

Lung Cancer Stage may access health-related information that you manually enter into the app for educational or reference use. This may include cancer staging inputs and related clinical descriptors, such as tumor characteristics, nodal status, metastatic status, histologic or anatomic details, or other information relevant to lung cancer stage determination.

This health-related information is entered by the user solely to allow the app to display staging results, reference content, and related educational information.

Whether We Collect or Receive Health Data

Bender Apps, LLC does not receive, collect, or store on its servers the health-related information that you enter into Lung Cancer Stage for staging calculations, provided the app is used only as intended within the app itself and without any external sharing features.

Health-related information entered into the app is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to us through the normal operation of the app.

How Health Data Is Used

Health-related information entered into Lung Cancer Stage is used only to:

  • Perform staging calculations or classifications within the app
  • Display educational and reference results based on user-entered inputs
  • Support the app’s clinical reference and learning functions

We do not use health-related information entered into the app for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

Sharing of Health Data

Bender Apps, LLC does not sell, rent, or share health-related information entered into Lung Cancer Stage with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

We do not disclose user-entered health-related information to third parties through the normal operation of the app.

Data Storage and Retention

Health-related information entered into Lung Cancer Stage is processed locally on the device. We do not maintain a remote database of this health-related information as part of the normal operation of the app.

If any app data is stored on the device to support app functionality, that storage remains local to the device unless the user separately exports, shares, backs up, or transmits it using device or third-party services outside our control.

Support Communications

Lung Cancer Stage does not collect personal information directly from users unless they voluntarily contact support by email.

If you email support, we may receive your email address, your name, and any other information you include in your message. That information is used only to respond to your inquiry, investigate technical issues, and improve the app.

Please do not send patient-identifiable information, protected health information, or other sensitive medical information by email.

Third-Party Services

Lung Cancer Stage does not use user-entered health-related information for third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing.

If you contact support by email, your message may be handled by your email provider and our email provider, each of which may process that communication according to its own privacy practices.

Health and Medical Disclaimer

Lung Cancer Stage is intended as an educational and reference tool only. It is not intended to provide diagnosis, treatment, or individualized medical advice, and it is not a substitute for professional medical judgment.

Children's Privacy

Lung Cancer Stage is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. Because user-entered health-related information is generally processed locally on the device, you can limit such use by discontinuing use of the app and clearing app data or uninstalling the app from your device, subject to your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
