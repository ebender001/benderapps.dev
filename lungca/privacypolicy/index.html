Lung Cancer Stage does not collect personal information directly from users unless they voluntarily contact support by email.

How Information Is Used

Lung Cancer Stage is intended as an educational and reference tool. It is not intended for diagnosis, treatment, or individualized medical advice.

Users should not submit protected health information or other sensitive patient data through support email.

If the app does not create accounts, sync data, or upload user content, then user data is generally processed locally on the device and not stored by us on external servers.

Third-Party Services

If Lung Cancer Stage uses third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, hosting, or email providers, those services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

If you do not use any such services, you may revise this section to state that the app does not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Lung Cancer Stage is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. If you have contacted support and want your support email removed from records you control, contact us at the email below.
