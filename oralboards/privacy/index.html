Privacy Policy for OralBoards AI

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

OralBoards AI is developed and operated by Bender Apps, LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

OralBoards AI may process information you provide while using the app, including spoken responses, speech transcriptions, simulated oral examination interactions, and AI-generated feedback.

This app collects anonymized usage data to improve performance and user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

We may also collect limited technical or usage information, such as app interactions, feature usage, crash logs, and performance information, to improve reliability and functionality.

Speech and AI Processing

The app uses speech recognition, text-to-speech, and third-party AI services to provide simulated oral board examinations and feedback. Your spoken or transcribed responses may be sent to these services solely to generate examiner prompts, evaluations, and case feedback.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your app data to train AI models.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your credit card or payment information.

Data Storage

Some app data may be stored locally on your device. Some data may be stored temporarily on secure servers to provide app functionality, maintain case sessions, generate feedback, improve performance, or troubleshoot errors.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate the app, such as AI, speech, hosting, analytics, or crash reporting providers, or when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide app functionality, improve the app, comply with legal obligations, or resolve technical issues.

Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. You may delete the app to remove locally stored data. You may also contact us to request deletion of any associated server-side data, where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

OralBoards AI is intended for medical trainees and physicians. It is not directed to children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact
